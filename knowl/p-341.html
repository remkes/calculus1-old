The leading coefficient in the numerator if \(8\text{,}\) and in the denominator is \(14\text{.}\) Only these terms matter, which radically simplifies the limit

\begin{equation*} \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{8x^4 + 3x^2 + 4}{14x^4 - 9x^3 - 50x^2 - 4x - 1} = \frac{8}{14} = \frac{4}{7} \end{equation*}
