The break even points are found by solving the cubic: \(x^3 -x^2-2x+1 - 3x = 0\text{.}\) The cubic doesn't factor nicely, but a computer can give us the approximate even \(x\) values: \(-1.25\text{,}\) \(0.45\) and \(1.81\text{.}\) We discard the negative value again. The largest break-even point is at approximately \(1.81\text{.}\)
