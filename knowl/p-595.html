We can use the fundamental theorem to calculate definite integrals as well. There is some special notation that is useful here: when we write \(\left. f(x) \right|_a^b\) we mean to evaluate \(f\) at \(a\) and \(b\) and subtract.

\begin{equation*} \left. f(x) \right|_a^b = f(b) - f(a) \end{equation*}
