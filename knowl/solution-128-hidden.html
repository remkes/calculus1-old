The indices and bounds of all the sums are the same, so we can combine them into one sum by taking the constants inside the sums and writing the additions and subtractions inside the sum. Notice there are two \(i^3\) terms, which we can combine into one.

\begin{align*} 19 \sum_{i=4}^{17} i^3 - \sum_{i=4}^{17} i^3 + 2 \sum_{i=4}^{17} i^2 - 36 \sum_{i=4}^{17} i \amp = \sum_{i=4}^{17} (19i^3 - i^3 + 2i^2 - 36 i) \\ \amp = \sum_{i=4}^{17} (18i^3 + 2i^2 - 36 i) \end{align*}
