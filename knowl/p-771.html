Before we get to the general process of curve sketching, we need to talk about interpretation of derivatives. We have seen in the previous lectures that the first derivative gives us information about increase and decrease. If \(f^\prime(x)\) is positive, the function is increasing, and if \(f^\prime(x)\) is negative, the function is decreasing. If \(f^\prime(x) = 0\text{,}\) then we may have a maximum or minimum.
