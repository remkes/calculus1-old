It looks like this data doubles every year, perhaps a little bit more than a year in some cases. We could guess a doubling period of \(\frac{11}{10}\text{.}\) With a starting value of 5, that gives us an exponential function.

\begin{equation*} f(x) = 5 e^{(\frac{10}{11}\ln 2)x} \end{equation*}
