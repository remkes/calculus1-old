Often is it important to recognize a composed functions and identify the pieces of the composition. The function \(h(x) = \sqrt{x+7}\) is a composition with inside function \(g(x) = x+7\) and outside function \(f(x) = \sqrt{x}\text{.}\) For complicated functions, there may be several ways to decompose the function as a composition; finding various decompositions and knowing which composition to use is an important skill.
