We take the limits from the left and from the right to determine continuity. The limit from the left is \(\frac{e}{16}\) from the right is \(\frac{39}{12}e^{\frac{10}{7}}\text{.}\) The approximate values of these numbers is, respectively, \(0.1699\) and \(13.5614\text{.}\) These are not the same, so the function is continuous.
