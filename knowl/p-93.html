Second, we think of functions as relationships and dependencies. When we have a function \(f: A \rightarrow B\text{,}\) we can thing of the elements of \(B\) depending on the elements of \(A\text{.}\) If we say that population growth is a function of food supply, we mean that there is a function which goes from numbers representing food suply to numbers representing growth rates. That function encodes the dependance of growth on food supply.
