In addition to the limit rules, there are two limits that are frequently useful. The second limit can be taken as a definition of the exponential base \(e\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} \amp = 1\\ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0^+} \left( 1 + x \right)^{\frac{1}{x}} \amp = e \end{align*}
