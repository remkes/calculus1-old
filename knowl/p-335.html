These are the most common types of functions we will consider. We can summarize this in a list of asymptotic orders: in the following list ‘\(f\lt g\)’ means that \(f\) grows slower than \(g\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} c \lt \ln x \lt \ldots \lt x^{\frac{1}{3}} \lt x^{\frac{1}{2}} \lt x \lt x^2 \lt x^3 \lt \ldots \lt e^x \lt x^x \lt \ldots \end{equation*}
